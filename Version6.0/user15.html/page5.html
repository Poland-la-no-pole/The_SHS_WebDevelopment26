Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about Invincible and this website.

What is Invincible?

Invincible is a superhero comic and animated series about Mark Grayson.

Who created Invincible?

The series was created by Robert Kirkman but was turned into art by Cory Walker and Ryan Ottley.

Is this an official website?

No, this is a fan-made school website.

Where can I watch Invincible?

It is available on streaming platforms like Amazon Prime Video, but there are comics available as well.

Is Invincible from Fortnite?

No, Invincible is a separate franchise from Fortnite.
