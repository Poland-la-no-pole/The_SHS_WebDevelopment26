Dungeons And Dragons

DND is a very fun game in my opinion, it is a very table top game that involves a party of people playing character they hav created. I normally play with my friends, i like being a dm and a player. i make alot of characters(look to the right) to play, my favorites are cairo kujo, corvus, and zakath because i put the most emotion into them. Cairo kujo is a ranger whose hunting clan was killed by other clans, Corvus is a monk who want to rebuild his fathers mob family, and zakath is a detective rogue.

  1. Monk,Paladin,Warlock,Ranger
  2. Barbarian,Bards,Cleric,
  3. Rogue,Fighter,Artificer,Druids
  4. Wizard,Sorcerer

The list to the right is my general ranking of the different classes, as you can see, i really like half casters. they feels so versatile and cool to use. i also like monk because who doesnt like being a punchy guy. i dislike full casters like wizards and sorcerers because it feels like they get so little beside spells and the spells dont even seem that good. their subclasses dont seem very good either. druids, bards, and clerics escape this since they get much more beside just spellcasting.
