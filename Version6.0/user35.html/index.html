From Flatbread to Global Icon

Exploring the ancient origins of the world's favorite food.

The Ancient Flatbread

Long before the 18th century, civilizations like the Greeks and Egyptians were eating flatbreads topped with oils and herbs. This is widely considered the precursor to what we know today as pizza.

Fun Fact: The word "pizza" was first documented in 997 AD in Gaeta, Italy!
Roman Focaccia

Panis focacius was a flatbread to which toppings were added.
