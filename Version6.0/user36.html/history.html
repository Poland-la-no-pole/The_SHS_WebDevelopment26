Minnesota Wild History

The Minnesota Wild have built their reputation over time through hard work, rebuilding years, and developing young talent. Their history shows how the team has grown into a consistent NHL competitor.

Major Moments

  • 2000 - The franchise begins its first NHL season
  • 2003 - The team makes an unexpected playoff run
  • 2007 - One of the strongest regular seasons in early franchise history
  • 2015 - A new core of young players begins to develop
  • 2020 - A breakout star emerges and changes the team’s future direction

Team Growth

Early Years

The team spent its early seasons building identity and learning how to compete in the NHL.

Modern Era

The team has focused on developing young talent and becoming a consistent playoff contender.

Evolution Highlights
