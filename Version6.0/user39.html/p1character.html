Character Info

Berserker or "Zerk" is an assassin character who excels at trading hits and using feint mix ups

Their main playstyle is centered around using "hard" feints to confuse your opponent while using hyper armor to trade hits.

Side note: hyper armor prevents flinching from an attack and having your current move canceled however you still take damage.
