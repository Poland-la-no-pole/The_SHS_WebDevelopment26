Characters

Meet the heroes, villains, and quirky supporting cast of Super Mario 3D Land — from a determined plumber to a spike-shelled tyrant.

The Heroes

Mario

The mustachioed plumber himself. Mario is the primary playable character — well-rounded with great jump height, solid running speed, and access to every power-up in the game.

His trademark move set — ground pound, long jump, side somersault — returns intact. In Tanooki form, his tail spin is devastating.

Playable Main Character

Luigi

Mario's taller, lankier brother is a post-game unlockable character. Luigi jumps higher than Mario but has less traction — sliding slightly on landings, making him trickier to control.

He also has slightly lower gravity on his jump arc, allowing more hang time. Perfect for advanced players who want an extra challenge.

Unlockable High Jump

Princess Peach

The princess of the Mushroom Kingdom, kidnapped by Bowser at the story's start. Peach plays a passive role in the main game but becomes a plot-critical character in the special worlds.

★ ★ ★

The Villains

Bowser — King of the Koopas

The primary antagonist. Bowser kidnaps Princess Peach and scatters the Super Leaves, triggering Mario's adventure. He appears at the end of every castle in an escalating series of battles.

His final fight in World 8's castle is a multi-stage epic — shoots fireballs in his first phase and when you get to his second phase he now shoots purple fireballs that deal the same amount of damge.

Bowser is defeated by hitting the button on the bridge's end.

Final Boss Recurring Villain

Boom Boom

Bowser's faithful enforcer, returning from Super Mario Bros. 3. Boom Boom guards various airships, spinning with his arms outstretched and charging at Mario relentlessly.

Defeated with three stomps, he falls dramatically each time — only to reappear stronger in a later world.

Sub-Boss

Pom Pom

A brand-new villain introduced in 3D Land — Pom Pom is Boom Boom's female counterpart. She throws boomerangs and leaps acrobatically across the battlefield.

Her clone ability in later appearances makes her one of the most challenging mid-bosses in the game.

Sub-Boss New to 3D Land!

Common Enemies

The Mushroom Kingdom is teeming with Bowser's foot soldiers. Here are the most iconic enemies you'll encounter:

Goomba

The humble foot soldier of Bowser's army. One stomp defeats them. In 3D Land, they also wear Tanooki Suits — making them glide at Mario!

Basic Enemy

Koopa Troopa

Red and Green variants patrol platforms. Stomping them produces a slideable shell — a classic tool for clearing out groups of enemies.

Shell Kick!

Boo

Ghost enemies that move when Mario looks away. They cluster in Ghost Houses and love to sneak up from behind in packs.

Spooky

Pokey

Multi-segmented cactus enemies that can only be safely stomped on the top head. Found in desert and lava stages.

Hazard

Hammer Bro

Elite troops who hurl hammers in arcs. Always appear in pairs and guard castle interiors. The Fire Flower is your best tool against them.

Elite!

Cheep-Cheep

Fish enemies from underwater and waterside stages. Some leap out of the water in parabolic arcs. Hard to dodge in narrow corridors.

Water Stage

Toad Houses

Toad Houses appear at the midpoint of each world. These friendly huts let Mario play a mini-game to earn items — inside each toad house you choose between 2 presents and you can only choose ONE present.

Inside one of the presents is one item but inside the other there is 4 items.
