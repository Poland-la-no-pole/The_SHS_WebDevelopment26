Why It's Special

Super Mario 3D Land was a ground-breaking experiment — it married the linear, goal-focused design of the classic 2D Mario games with the free-roaming exploration of Super Mario 64 and Super Mario Galaxy.

The result? A game that felt instantly familiar yet totally fresh. Levels are compact but dense with secrets, timing challenges, and clever use of the 3DS's stereoscopic 3D effect.

With over 16 worlds (8 main + 8 special), two playable characters, and a massive post-game, there's always something new to discover.
