The Story

A stormy night, a stolen princess, and a determined plumber — follow Mario on his most personal rescue mission yet.

🌪️ A Dark & Stormy Night

In the Mushroom Kingdom, a massive Super Leaf Tree stood proudly near Peach's Castle — its leaves scattered across the land, giving rise to countless Tanooki Suits.

Then one night, a violent storm swept through. When morning came, the tree was stripped bare — and Princess Peach had vanished.

A calling card was left behind. Bowser, King of the Koopas, had struck again.

Story Chapters

Mario discovers a postcard from Bowser — a taunt showing Peach locked in a cage with the stolen Super Leaves. Furious and determined, Mario sets off across the first worlds.

The early worlds serve as a warm-up: Bowser's minions guard rolling hills, desert dunes, and underground caves. Mario must recover Star Medals to progress.

Worlds 1–2

Mario storms through airships, haunted ghost houses, and scorching lava castles. Bowser sends his Koopalings and elite troops to stop the plumber at every turn.

At the end of each world's castle, a massive boss awaits — and each Bowser encounter raises the stakes. Mario must use his doging abilitys to deflect fireballs and reach the button.

Worlds 3–5

Mario battles through treacherous icy peaks and sky-high towers. Finally, he reaches Bowser's massive fortress — a multi-stage gauntlet of fire, traps, and acrobatics.

The climax sees a massive Bowser battle across a crumbling bridge over a lava pit. Mario must dodge fireballs to hit the final button.

Worlds 6–8

After rescuing Peach, a surprise twist reveals Luigi has been captured! Mario must power through 8 brutal Special Worlds — remixed levels with new challenges and harder enemies.
