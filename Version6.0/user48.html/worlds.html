Worlds & Levels

From grassy plains to lava-filled fortresses — every world in Super Mario 3D Land offers a new theme, new enemies, and new surprises.

🌍 The 16-World Journey

The game is split into 8 Main Worlds and 8 Special Worlds, each containing multiple levels, a midpoint Toad House, and a fortress or castle boss.

Every level hides 3 Star Medals. Collecting enough unlocks the next world's path — and gathering them all opens the ultimate challenge in the Special Worlds.

Main Worlds (1–8)

World 1 — Grassy Plains

Mario's adventure begins in sunny green fields. Simple moving platforms, basic Goombas, and the first Tanooki Leaf introduce core mechanics.

The castle boss is a typical bowser fight

Beginner

World 2 — Desert Dunes

Sandy pyramids, quicksand pits, and Pokey cacti fill this sun-scorched world. Watch for hidden underground caverns!

Boss: Boom Boom, returning from Super Mario Bros. 3.

Easy

World 3 — Waterside Cliffs

Waterfalls, slippery wet platforms, and Cheep-Cheeps make this world a wet and wild ride. The second airship level appears here.

Moderate

World 4 — Ghost Houses

Dark corridors, illusion rooms, and Boos lurk around every corner. Levels flip and rotate to disorient the player.

Spooky!

World 5 — Sky Jungles

High-altitude platforms over endless drops, vine-covered ruins, and Piranha Plants on every ledge. Precision jumping is key.

Moderate

World 6 — Icy Peaks

Slippery ice physics meet treacherous narrow ledges. Freezing winds knock Mario off course. Spike Traps everywhere.

Hard

World 7 — Lava Fortress

Magma rises beneath crumbling stone bridges. Hammer Bros. and Lakitus rain projectiles. The pressure is relentless.

Very Hard

World 8 — Bowser's Castle

The final gauntlet combines every mechanic from all previous worlds. Multiple sub-areas lead to the ultimate Bowser showdown.

Extreme!

Special Worlds (S1–S8)

After rescuing Princess Peach, eight brutal Special Worlds unlock. These are remixed, harder versions of existing levels — with more enemies, fewer platforms, and brutal time limits.

Completing all Special Worlds and collecting every Star Medal unlocks the Crown on your save file — the ultimate badge of honour for Mario masters.
