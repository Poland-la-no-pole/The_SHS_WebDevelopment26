About Our Project

Our project focuses on solving the problem of inconsistent plant care. Many people forget to water plants regularly because of busy schedules, vacations, or lack of experience with gardening. The Smart Greenhouse System uses technology to automate the watering process. By using moisture sensors and water pumps, plants can receive water only when needed. This system can help reduce water waste, improve plant health, and make gardening easier for homes, schools, and greenhouses.

Project Goals

  • Create a system that automatically waters plants.
  • Reduce wasted water.
  • Improve plant growth consistency.
  • Make gardening easier for beginners.
