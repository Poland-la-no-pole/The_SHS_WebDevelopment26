Project Gallery

Below are examples of greenhouse technology, irrigation systems, moisture sensors, and healthy plant growth.

Robotic Spray

Automated spray robots for plants are precision machines that navigate fields or greenhouses to apply pesticides, herbicides, and nutrients

Pollinator Robot

Pollinator robots perform flower inspection, mapping, pollination, and development tracking.

Harvesting Robot

Harvesting robots accurately identify ripe versus unripe plants, harvest them, and place them in on-board boxing systems.

Spacing Robots

Spacing robots provide more consistent spacing and increase quality by optimizing plant placement and reducing the amount of water, pesticides, herbicides, and fertilizers used.

Vertical Hydroponic

Vertical growing can maximize space and double or triple the inventory within your greenhouse since you’re using nearly every square foot when you garden up-and-down, versus side-to-side.

Automatic Watering

Drones are revolutionizing controlled-environment agriculture by automating labor-intensive greenhouse tasks.
