The Game of Hockey

Hockey is a fast-paced, thrilling sport played on ice between two teams of six players.

How the Game Works

Each team tries to score by shooting a rubber puck into the opposing team's net. Players use their sticks to pass, shoot, and handle the puck. A typical game consists of three 20-minute periods with two 15-minute intermissions.

Key Rules

  • Offsides - A player cannot be ahead of the puck when it enters the offensive zone
  • Icing - Shooting the puck from behind the center line down the ice is not allowed unless behind in score
  • Penalties - Breaking rules results in a player sitting in the penalty box for 2-5 minutes
  • Goals - Shooting the puck completely across the goal line counts as one point

Positions

Forwards

Attackers focused on scoring and setting up plays.

Defensemen

Protectors focused on defending the net.

Goaltender

Net protector with special equipment and rules.
