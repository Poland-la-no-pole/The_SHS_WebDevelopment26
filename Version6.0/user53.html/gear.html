Hockey Gear

Essential equipment needed to play hockey safely and effectively.

🏑 Sticks

Hockey sticks are used to shoot, pass, and handle the puck. They come in different curves and flex ratings suitable for different playing styles.

⛸️ Skates

Specialized ice skates designed for hockey with reinforced ankles and blades optimized for quick movement, acceleration, and sharp turns.

🪖 Helmet

A protective helmet with a face cage or shield to protect the head and face from pucks, sticks, and collisions during play.

🧤 Gloves

Padded gloves that protect the hands and wrists while allowing players to maintain control of the stick and puck.

🛡️ Pads

Protective pads for shoulders, elbows, knees, and shins to prevent injuries during collisions and play.

⚫ Puck

A hard rubber disk, 3 inches in diameter and 1 inch thick, used as the object of play in hockey.
