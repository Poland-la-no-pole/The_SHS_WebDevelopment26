The Stanley Cup

The Stanley Cup is the most prestigious award in professional ice hockey. It is awarded annually to the champion of the Stanley Cup Finals, concluding the NHL playoffs.

History

First awarded in 1892 to the Montreal Hockey Club, the Cup has been the symbol of excellence in hockey for over a century. Teams compete fiercely every season for the honor of hoisting this iconic trophy.

The Cup Today
