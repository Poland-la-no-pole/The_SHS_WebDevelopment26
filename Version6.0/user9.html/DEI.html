Doofenshmirtz Evil Incorporated

Doofenshmirtz Evil Incorporated is the fictional, villainous corporation owned by Dr. Heinz Doofenshmirtz in Phineas and Ferb, headquartered in a tall, purple tower in Danville. It serves as his base to create "-inator" inventions for taking over the Tri-State Area, primarily funded by alimony from his ex-wife, Charlene.
