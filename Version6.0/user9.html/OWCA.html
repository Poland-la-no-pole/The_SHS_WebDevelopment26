OWCA Main Staff

Major Monogram

Major Franics Monogram

Major Francis Monogram is a supporting character in Phineas and Ferb and he is the head of the Tri-State Area Branch and Perry's (Agent P) commanding officer. His role is to give Perry his missions each episode.

Carl Karl

Carl Karl

Carl Karl is unpaid intern and cameraman for Major Francis Monogram at the OWCA where he handles technical administrative tasks, assists with agent briefings, and acts as an overall sidekick all while he is pursuing college credit.

Admiral Wanda Acronym

Admiral Wanda Acronym

Admiral Wanda Acronym is a high-ranking leader in the OWCA, serving as a divison leader who commands her own team of animal agents. She functions as the commanding officer for other agents like Major Monogram does for Perry the Platypus(Agent P).

Phineas and Ferb

Phineas and Ferb follows two inventive stepbrothers executing grand-scale engineering projects throughout their summer vacation. Simultaneously, their pet platypus operates as a secret agent to neutralize the threats posed by the eccentric Dr. Heinz Doofenshmirtz.
