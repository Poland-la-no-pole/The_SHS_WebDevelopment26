Tornado Safety

Make sure you are in a safe place and your getting informed about the tornado

Safety Tips

  • Go to a basement or small room on the lowest floor of your house
  • Stay away from your windows
  • Wear a helmet
  • Cover yourself with a blanket or a mattress
  • Bring a phone or weather radio

Supplies

  • Water
  • Non perishable food
  • Flashlight
  • Batteries
  • First aid kit
  • Phone charger

Warning Signs

Signs What it Means
Green or dark sky A very strong storm is forming
Big hail Powerful storm coming
Roaring sound A tornado might be close
Rotating clouds Tornado possibly forming

Watch vs Warning

Term Meaning
Tornado Watch Conditions are right for a tornado to form
Tornado Warning A tornado formed and been seen
