How Tornadoes Form

Steps

  1. Warm air rises
  2. Cold/dry air comes in from another direction
  3. Wind makes the air start to spin
  4. The storm grows forms a funnel
  5. If the funnel touches the ground, then it becomes a tornado

Ingredients

  • Moisture
  • Instability
  • Lift
  • Wind shear

Terms

Wind Shear

A sudden change in wind speed or direction

Supercell

A rotating storm that can make strong tornadoes

Funnel Cloud

A rotating cloud that hasnt touched the ground yet
