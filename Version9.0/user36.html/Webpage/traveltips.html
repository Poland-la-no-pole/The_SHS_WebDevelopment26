Hawaii Travel Tips

Everything you need to know before visiting the islands

🏖 Respect Beaches

Always respect posted signs, avoid protected areas, and never take sand or rocks from Hawaiian beaches.

🌋 Stay Safe Near Volcanoes

Follow park rules at Hawaii Volcanoes National Park. Stay on marked trails and never approach lava flows.

🌴 Respect Local Culture

Learn basic Hawaiian words like “Aloha” and “Mahalo.” Be respectful of sacred sites and traditions.

🌊 Ocean Safety

Hawaiian waters can have strong currents. Always check surf warnings before swimming.

🚗 Driving Tips

Roads can be narrow and winding. Take your time and enjoy scenic routes like Road to Hana.

☀️ Sun Protection

Hawaii sun is very strong year-round. Use sunscreen, hats, and stay hydrated.
