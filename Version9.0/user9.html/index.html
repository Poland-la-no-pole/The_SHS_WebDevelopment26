Phineas and Ferb

Phineas and Ferb is a popular animated musical-comedy series created by Dan Povenmire and Jeff "Swampy" Marsh that aired on Disney Channel from 2007 to 2015 and a revival that aired on June 5, 2025 with additional content for 2026. The show follows the daily adventures of two ingenius stepbrothers, Phineas Flynn and Ferb Fletcher as they make many inventions and do different activities during their very long summer vacation.

Phineas and Ferb

Phineas and Ferb follows two inventive stepbrothers executing grand-scale engineering projects throughout their summer vacation. Simultaneously, their pet platypus operates as a secret agent to neutralize the threats posed by the eccentric Dr. Heinz Doofenshmirtz.
